Happiness

2016/8/7

Three ways:

  • Tack action

  • Visualization

    大脑无法分辨想象与事实。但是他会去遗忘不愉快的事。
    不仅要想象结果，还要去想象过程中的行动。
    理论+例子（I have a dream）
    情感与行动的联系，you have to evoke emotion to create motion.

  • Cognitive Therapy 认知疗法

    Thoughts drive emotion.
    Event->Evaluation/thought->Emotion
    改变对事情的看法。

    Where do I distort the reality? Where do I have distortion in terms of evaluation, in terms of my thought. That lead to certain negative emotions that are unnecessary?

    Over generalization. 应该实事求是。
    All or nothing. Mining. 极小化 “隧道视野”
    每个人都会遇到不顺心的事情，关键是你怎么面对，怎么快速的从失落的情绪中转移出来。
